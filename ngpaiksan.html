Ng Paik San

黄白山

Born 1982

Ng Paik San is a Sarawakian artist known for his layered, expressive works that blend traditional Bornean motifs with modern design. Using acrylic, watercolour, and mixed media, his art explores cultural memory, heritage, and the evolving identity of Sarawak in a globalized world.
