Stephanie Eng

史蒂芬妮·黄

Born 1985

Stephanie Eng is a contemporary Sarawak-born artist celebrated for her vibrant explorations of Borneo’s cultural heritage and landscapes. A graduate of Universiti Malaysia Sarawak, she is known for combining traditional motifs with modern techniques, creating work that bridges generations and traditions.
