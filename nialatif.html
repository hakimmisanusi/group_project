Nia Latif

妮娅·拉蒂夫

Born 1990

Nia Latif is a contemporary Sarawakian artist whose work explores identity, heritage, and nature through vibrant mixed media and abstract styles. Inspired by Borneo’s diverse cultures and lush landscapes, Nia’s art combines traditional patterns with modern forms, offering a fresh, meaningful perspective on Sarawak’s evolving cultural narrative.
