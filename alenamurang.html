Alena Murang

艾琳娜·慕朗

Born 1989

Alena Murang is a Sarawakian artist and musician celebrated for her mission to preserve and share Borneo’s cultural heritage. Her visual art often features Dayak motifs, ancestral stories, and lush natural scenes, rendered in acrylic and mixed media. Through her art and music, Alena bridges past and present, inviting viewers to engage with the rich traditions of Sarawak.
