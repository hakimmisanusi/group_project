Sheila Kho

谢丽娅·许

Born 1988

Sheila Kho is a Sarawakian artist celebrated for her poetic, contemplative artworks that draw inspiration from Borneo’s natural beauty and cultural heritage. Using watercolour and mixed media, she creates layered, evocative compositions exploring memory, identity, and the intricate relationship between tradition and modern life.
