Marzuki Jamilah

马祖基·贾米拉

Born 1984

Marzuki Jamilah is a Sarawakian artist celebrated for his powerful, modern interpretations of Borneo’s cultural heritage. Using acrylic and mixed media, he fuses traditional Dayak and Malay patterns with contemporary design, creating works that explore identity, memory, and the vibrant spirit of Sarawak’s communities.
