REBIRTH: Past and Present

Hoan Gallery, by Kee Huang, is a passion project of patience and paint. Discover the works of Sarawakian artists, past and present, in our opening exhibition.

The Artists

Stephanie Eng

Contemporary artist specialising in watercolour and acrylic.

Chong Liew Syn (1949–1999)

Renowned for traditional oil painting capturing Sarawak landscapes.

Foo Syn Choon (1933–2012)

Veteran artist celebrated for expressive Borneo heritage scenes.

Lam Siong Onn

Master of watercolour, documenting Sarawak’s people and places.

Chee Hong King (1920–1992)

Influential early Sarawak painter known for naturalistic styles.

Nia Latif

Modern abstract painter exploring cultural symbolism.

Leonard Siaw

Urban realist painter famed for street murals and portraiture.

Michael Chong (1948–2021)

Traditional artist with works highlighting Sarawak’s rivers and life.

Lee Hock Kia

Known for expressive ink and watercolour landscapes.

Hoan Kee Huang

Gallery founder, painter of layered Sarawakian stories.

Narong Daun

Artist working in mixed media reflecting Borneo’s heritage.

David Chew

Painter focused on human figures and local narratives.

Marzuki Jamilah

Contemporary expressionist with bold, emotive brushwork.

Sheila Kho

Artist and educator celebrating culture through vibrant colours.

Ng Paik San

Focused on Sarawak’s nature through fine, detailed work.

Alena Murang

Musician and artist painting indigenous Dayak heritage.

Bong Choo Chew

Veteran artist of landscapes and traditional Borneo life.
