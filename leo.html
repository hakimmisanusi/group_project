Leonard Siaw

萧志强

Born 1987

Leonard Siaw is a Sarawakian artist renowned for his expressive urban sketches and watercolour paintings that capture the cultural soul of Kuching and Borneo. Known for documenting heritage buildings, bustling markets, and street life with fluid lines and vivid washes, Leonard’s work celebrates Sarawak’s unique character and preserves its disappearing historical landscapes.
