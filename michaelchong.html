Michael Chong

张美豪

Born 1965

Michael Chong is a prominent Sarawakian artist best known for his evocative watercolour paintings that celebrate the landscapes, kampung scenes, and daily life of Borneo. With masterful use of colour and light, his art preserves Sarawak’s natural beauty and cultural heritage, capturing the timeless charm of rivers, forests, and traditional villages.
