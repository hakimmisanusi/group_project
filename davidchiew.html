David Chiew

周大伟

Born 1982

David Chiew is a Sarawakian artist celebrated for his dynamic, colourful depictions of Borneo’s landscapes, cultural heritage, and daily life. Using acrylic and watercolour, his paintings capture the energy and beauty of Sarawak’s rivers, markets, and traditional villages, offering viewers a vivid and contemporary interpretation of local identity and scenery.
