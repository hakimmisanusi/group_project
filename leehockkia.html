Lee Hock Kia

李福佳

Born 1947

Lee Hock Kia is a distinguished Sarawakian artist renowned for his masterful watercolour paintings that vividly capture the spirit of kampung life, bustling markets, and traditional shophouses. With fine detail and rich, warm colours, his works preserve Sarawak’s heritage and evoke a deep nostalgia for the timeless scenes of local culture and daily life.
