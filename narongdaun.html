Narong Daun

纳荣·道恩

Born 1975

Narong Daun is a Sarawakian artist renowned for his dynamic paintings inspired by Dayak culture, traditional motifs, and Borneo’s natural beauty. Working primarily in acrylic and mixed media, Narong blends ancestral symbolism with contemporary technique, celebrating Sarawak’s rich heritage while offering modern interpretations of identity and tradition.
