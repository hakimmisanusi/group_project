Lam Siong Onn

蓝祥安

Born 1950

Lam Siong Onn is a renowned Sarawakian artist celebrated for his masterful watercolour paintings that capture the essence of local landscapes, riverscapes, and village life. With decades of experience, Lam’s works are known for their striking realism and delicate play of light, preserving the heritage and beauty of Sarawak for generations.
